Their life is hanging in the balance.
Be the counterweight.

Since the beginning of time the ocean has worked diligently to keep our earth and atmosphere clean and functioning. But it can’t keep up. Daily carbon emissions are at all-time highs. The result is a changed ocean chemistry a process called acidification. The impact is significant. Oysters clams corals and animals aren’t getting what they need from the ocean to form their shells and skeletons. This has wide-ranging consequences for them for the ocean’s food chain and for the thousands of fishermen who make their livelihoods from their existence.

The time to act is now. Donate to protect the sea.

DID YOU KNOW? 79 million tons of CO2 spews into the atmosphere every day.

We know cement production, deforestation and the burning of fossil fuels are the primary culprits making our oceans more acidic. Which is why we need to start at the source. That means strong commitments from local and federal governments to curb carbon emissions. And strong commitments from you to help us influence governments to step up to protect the sea. Come onboard with us to shore up the will of local and federal governments to do their part for ocean health.

Make a splash for a cleaner ocean. Donate now.

Science is the solution

When it comes to the health of the ocean, science holds the key. We need scientists working to find alternatives for fossil fuels, studying strategies for removing carbon dioxide from the water and researching other solutions for keeping our ocean clean and its inhabitants thriving. Your support makes all of this happen.

You can make a difference Come aboard with us as we work to influence governments to do more to protect the world’s oceans. Take a stand with science to protect the sea, its inhabitants and the livelihoods that rely on it.
