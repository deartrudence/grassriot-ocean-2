Give now to support young women dealing with breast cancer.

Rethink exists to empower young women concerned about or diagnosed with breast cancer wherever they are in the world and in the moments they need it most.

For everyone affected by breast cancer and the people who love them, your support is essential. Donate now.

You can be a part of the change as we break through stigmas and bring together young women - transforming isolation into an empowered community. Because we know there's power in numbers.

Your gift right now ensures that we improve the lives of young women by providing:

  • Education

    So that young women will have the information they need to be breast aware and risk savvy.

  • Resources + Community

    That support the unique issues young women living with breast cancer face.

  • Research

    That aims to answer some of the unique questions surrounding young women with breast cancer.

  • Advocacy

    To improve breast cancer services, treatment, education and research for young women with cancer.

  • Awareness

    Through bold, attention-grabbing campaigns we spread our mission in away that gets people talking.

If we can change the course of breast cancer for young women, we will change its course for everyone.

Every day, we seek creative new ways to boldly engage young people with the cause. Our inititatives are as cutting-edge as the minds behind them and as unique as the people we reach with them. No project or campaign is too big or small for our team to take on, as long as it fills a gap for young people concerned about or affected by breast cancer.
