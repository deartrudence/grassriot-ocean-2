    Ecojustice is Canada's only national environmental law charity. We are 100% donor-funded and have a 25-year track record of winning legal victories for people and the planet.

    25 Years of victories

    Highlights

    • 1992 halted logging in Wood Buffalo National Park
    • 1994 helped the James Bay Cree protect the Great Whale River
    • 1998 stopped logging of old growth forests in Ontario
    • 2000 forced Hamilton to clean-up pollution
    • 2003 helped hold Imperial Oil to account for contamination
    • 2013 pressured Nestlé to limit Ontario water withdrawals
