If we don't act now to protect our important natural landscapes, they may be lost forever.

A donation to NCC will help ensure that the Canadian wilderness remains wild and free.

Since 1962, we've helped conserve more than 2.8 million acres (1.1 million hectares) of ecologically signifigant land across the country. Each year this number grows as we protect new conservation areas, all to create a conservation legacy for nature, for now, forever.

Join the 200,000 supporters from Canada and around the world who are helping us protect our natural spaces, and the species that rely on them. Donate Now!

In the past year we sucessfully met our conservation goals, and we have the numbers to prove it.

  • Protected 21,105 acres (8,451 hectares) over the course of the year.
  • Conserved 100% of our new properties within a Natural Area.
  • Welcomed 145 new Nature Legacy Society members, individuals who will leave NCC a bequest in their Wills.
  • Hosted 253 Conservation Volunteers events, attracting 2,650 participants.
  • Hosted close to 70 research projects on our conservation lands.
