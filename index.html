    Ecojustice is Canada's only national environmental law charity. We are 100% donor-funded and have a 25-year track record of winning legal victories for people and the planet.

    25 Years of victories

    Highlights

    • 1992 halted logging in Wood Buffalo National Park
    • 1994 helped the James Bay Cree protect the Great Whale River
    • 1998 stopped logging of old growth forests in Ontario
    • 2000 forced Hamilton to clean-up pollution
    • 2003 helped hold Imperial Oil to account for contamination in Quebec
    • 2013 pressured Nestlé to limit Ontario water withdrawals

    Donate for a democratic, fair, and responsible Canada

    Since the Leadnow.ca community was founded in 2011 we’ve grown to include over 400,000 people across Canada through campaigns to hold government accountable and create a strong democracy, fair economy and safe climate for all generations.

    Despite all the incredible, dedicated work we’ve done together, the truth is we can’t achieve real progress on the issues that matter if vote splitting gives the Harper Conservatives the power of another “majority” government.

    Time and again, this government has shown that it’s deeply out of line with the values the Leadnow community, and the majority of people across Canada, hold dear. Values like fairness, openness, defending our environment, building strong public health care, and reducing inequality.

    That’s why thousands of people from the Leadnow community have kick-started a people-powered election campaign to mobilize hundreds of thousands across Canada to vote together, defeat the Harper Conservatives, and move Canada forward with real progress for our democracy, economy and environment.

    The campaign is already building incredible momentum, but the Harper Conservatives are building strength in the polls and there’s growing speculation that the election could be called in late winter or early spring.

    Your donations will:

    • Build volunteer-run Community Action Teams in battleground ridings from coast to coast to coast. We know swing ridings can be won with strong teams of people knocking on doors, recruiting people to vote together, and then getting out the vote on election day. Teams are already forming in 15 key ridings and we need your donations to providing crucial training, communication materials and strategic support.
    • Create an online hub to map out the best ways for thousands of us to have an impact in the election. If we raise enough money to fully fund this project, then in the lead-up to the election we’ll be able to send everyone who has signed the Vote Together pledge up to date information about the state of play in their riding, and the best way they can have an impact - whether that’s in your riding, or calling into a swing riding to get out the vote.
    • Support hard-hitting campaigns to hold candidates from all parties accountable for real progress. We need to keep speaking out to show all the candidates that the Leadnow community is a growing force to be reckoned with. Regardless of who wins the election, we’ll need to take action together for real progress to strengthen our democracy, build a fair economy and do our part to stop runaway climate change.

    If you can, please consider a monthly pledge. By donating monthly, you will lay a strong foundation for our major campaign in the lead-up to the 2015 election and beyond.

    2015 is going to be a watershed year. This government has spent years taking actions that have moved Canada backwards, and we need new leadership. More importantly, we need to build a critical mass of citizens who will continue working together to build an open democracy, create a fair economy and ensure a safe climate for all generations.

    Let’s build that movement together.
